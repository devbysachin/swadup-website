Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is SwadUp?
SwadUp is a brand offering premium-quality food and beverage products focused on taste, hygiene, and satisfaction.

2. How can I place an order?
Visit our website, select your product, add it to cart, and proceed to checkout.

3. What payment methods do you accept?
UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, and COD (in select areas).

4. How long does delivery take?
Usually 3–7 business days depending on location.

5. Can I cancel my order?
Only before the order is shipped.

6. How do I track my order?
Go to “Track Order” on our website and enter your order ID.

7. Is COD available?
Yes, in select regions (checked at checkout).

8. Do you offer bulk orders?
Yes. Use the “Get in Touch” page for bulk inquiries.

9. What if I receive a wrong/damaged product?
Raise a return request with images within 48 hours of delivery.
