Return & Refund Policy

Returns Eligibility:
Items must be unused, sealed, and requested within 48 hours. Perishable items are non-returnable unless incorrect or damaged.

How to Initiate a Return:
1. Visit the Return & Refund page
2. Fill the return form with order ID and photo proof
3. Pickup scheduled in 2–5 business days

Refunds:
Once return is inspected, refund is processed in 5–7 business days. COD customers will be contacted for bank details.

Non-Returnable Items:
Opened/used products, perishable goods, or items damaged by customer.

Exchange:
Only allowed for damaged/defective products. Contact us via “Get in Touch”.
