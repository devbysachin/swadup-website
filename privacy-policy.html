Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2025

At SwadUp, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping address, and payment details during checkout.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, IP address, browser type, and device info via cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To send order confirmations, delivery updates, and customer support
  • To improve our website, user experience, and product offerings
  • To send promotional emails or updates (only if you opt-in)

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Trusted delivery and logistics partners
  • Payment gateways for transaction processing
  • Legal authorities if required by law

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to personalize content, analyze traffic, and understand user behavior. You can control cookie settings in your browser at any time.

5. Data Security

Your data is stored securely and protected through encryption and security protocols. However, no method of transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

  • You may access, update, or delete your data anytime by contacting us.
  • You can opt-out of marketing emails through unsubscribe links provided.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to read their privacy policies separately.

8. Children’s Privacy

SwadUp does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
